/ Quick Answers

Common Questions

Before you reach out, here are answers to the questions we get asked most often.

How far in advance should I book BL Events?

We recommend booking at least 3–6 months in advance for weddings and large events. For corporate events and birthday parties, 4–6 weeks is usually sufficient. However, we can accommodate urgent bookings depending on availability — reach out and we'll let you know.

Do you manage events outside Rajkot?

Absolutely. BL Events covers all 33 districts of Gujarat and all 11 cities of Saurashtra. We also take outstation events across India on request. Our on-ground coordinators travel with the team to ensure seamless execution anywhere.

Is the initial consultation free?

Yes — completely free. Our first consultation involves understanding your vision, requirements, and budget. We then prepare a customised proposal within 48 hours at no charge and with no commitment on your part.

Can we customise our event package?

Every BL Events package is fully customisable. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all. Tell us your vision, your budget, and your must-haves — and we'll design a proposal that fits perfectly.

What's the minimum budget to work with BL Events?

There's no fixed minimum — we work with budgets of all sizes. Small intimate gatherings and large-scale concerts both receive the same level of care and professionalism. Tell us your budget and we'll tell you exactly what's possible.

Do you handle photography and décor together?

Yes — BL Events is a full-service agency. We have an in-house photography and cinematography team alongside our décor specialists, so you don't need to coordinate multiple vendors. Everything is handled by one team, ensuring perfect sync on your event day.

How do I get a quote or proposal?

Simply fill out the inquiry form above or call us directly. Once we understand your requirements, our team prepares a detailed, itemised proposal within 48 hours — completely free with no obligation to proceed.

Do you do celebrity bookings and live shows?

Yes. Our Celebrity Management division has organized events with Bollywood artists, regional folk stars, motivational speakers, and more. We handle artist booking, contracts, travel, hospitality, and on-day coordination end to end.
