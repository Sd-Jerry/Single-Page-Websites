Our Story

A Trusted Name in Beauty & Skincare

Located in Rajkot, Gujarat, Care & Glow – The Beauty Studio has been a trusted name in beauty and skincare for the past 5 years. We specialize in delivering high-quality beauty services right to your doorstep, ensuring both comfort and convenience for our clients.

Our services include hand & leg waxing, eyebrow shaping, nail care, facial treatments, relaxing massages, hair styling, and professional side makeup. Every service is performed using branded, skin-friendly products while maintaining strict hygiene standards, so you can enjoy a safe and luxurious experience every time.

We believe beauty should be effortless and accessible. That's why we also offer free home service on large bookings, making it easier for you to relax and get pampered without stepping out.

To ensure the best service experience, we operate on an appointment basis. You can easily book your slot through our WhatsApp number or by filling out the contact form on our website.
