/ Our Story

A Decade of Excellence in Every Event

BL Events was founded in Rajkot in 2014 with a single belief — that every celebration deserves to be extraordinary, regardless of scale. What started as a small team of passionate event professionals has grown into one of Gujarat's most recognized event management companies.

Over the past decade, we have planned and executed over 250 events across Saurashtra and Gujarat — from intimate family gatherings to grand weddings with 1000+ guests, from high-energy live concerts to precision-organized corporate summits.

We are not just event planners. We are storytellers, designers, and memory-makers — and every event we touch carries the BL Events signature of elegance, creativity, and flawless execution.

Headquartered in Rajkot with active operations across all of Gujarat and Saurashtra.

/ Our Journey

A Decade of Milestones

Every great company has a story. Here's ours — ten years of growth, learning, and unforgettable events that shaped who we are today.

2014

The Beginning

BL Events was founded in Rajkot with a 3-person team and a single mission — to make premium event planning accessible to everyone in Saurashtra.

2015

First 25 Events

Completed our first 25 weddings and social events across Rajkot, Jamnagar, and Junagadh. Word-of-mouth referrals became our strongest growth engine.

2017

Corporate Division Launch

Expanded into corporate event management, organizing annual galas, product launches, and leadership conferences for businesses across Gujarat.

2019

Live Shows & Celebrity Events

Entered the live entertainment space — organizing our first large-scale musical night with 5,000+ attendees in Rajkot. Celebrity management division established.

2021

Photography Wing Added

Launched BL Events Photography & Cinematography — an in-house team dedicated to capturing every emotion, every frame, every memory.

2024

250+ Events Milestone

Crossed 250 successfully executed events and expanded our team to 25+ professionals, with coverage across all 33 districts of Gujarat. The journey continues.
